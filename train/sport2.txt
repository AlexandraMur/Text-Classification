BATE Borisov wins Belarus’ Beach Football Super Cup

BATE Borisov has won the Beach Football Super Cup of Belarus in a match against the Minsk State Pedagogical University team, 4-3.

Prior to the European Cup scheduled to be held in Italy’s Catania on 23 May, BATE will once again test its strength in the first round of Belarus’ Championship in Mozyr on 14 May.

BATE’s European Cup rivals in group A along with the tournament hosts will be Greece’s Atlas and Zwolle of the Netherlands.
